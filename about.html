Our Journey in Crafting Excellence

Founded in 2010, Galaxee Flowmate began as a small workshop dedicated to redefining bathroom aesthetics. What started as a passion project between three design enthusiasts has blossomed into an internationally recognized brand.

We've spent over a decade perfecting the balance between form and function, creating bath accessories that don't just serve a purpose but elevate the entire bathroom experience.
